Education

Dr. Radošević, who for many years headed the medical service in the Šmarješke Toplice resort, entered his professional career in 1986 after graduating from the Faculty of Medicine in Zagreb. Danilo Radoshevich came to the Dolenjske Toplice resort on a scholarship from the Krka company. In 1995 he passed the exam in the specialty of internal medicine, and in 1996 he studied at the school of densitometry in Portland, USA. In the same year, he organized a clinic for diagnosing osteoporosis by measuring bone mineral density using DXA devices in the Dolenjske Toplice resort, and to date he has carried out about 50,000 such examinations.
