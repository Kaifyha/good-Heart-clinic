Helga Kyper

surgeon of the highest category

Education

Cardiac surgeon "Cardioliths" and the Cardiac Surgery Clinic of Vilnius University. The main specialization is adult heart surgery: the whole range of modern cardiac surgeries performed for various heart pathologies. The main ones: coronary artery bypass surgery with and without artificial circulation, i.e. operations on a beating heart, prosthetics and plastic surgery of the heart valves, operations to restore the volume and geometry of the left ventricle in the presence of post-infarction aneurysm and significantly impaired left ventricular function. Carries out about 200 operations per year of varying degrees of complexity, incl. and especially complex.

Practic

Trained in the USA, England, Sweden. He took part in cardiac surgery congresses in Vilnius, Riga, Tallinn, Frankfurt, Helsinki, Glasgow, Cancun, made presentations at the events of the European Society of Cardiac Surgeons. The main topic of scientific research and reports is the restoration of the geometry of the left ventricle.
